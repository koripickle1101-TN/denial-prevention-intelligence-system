Claim Quality Control

Claim Readiness Checklist

A pre-submission checklist for reducing preventable denial risk before the claim leaves the organization.

Checklist

Readiness GateQuestion
EligibilityIs coverage active for the date of service?
AuthorizationDoes authorization match service, date range, and payer requirement?
DocumentationIs medical necessity support available?
CodingDo diagnosis and procedure/service codes align?
DemographicsAre member identifiers and patient demographics clean?
Payer RulesAre payer-specific submission rules satisfied?
Final ReviewIs the claim ready for submission without preventable defects?

Executive Rule

Claim readiness should be confirmed before submission, not after the denial arrives.
