Denial Prevention Intelligence System

A healthcare revenue cycle portfolio project for identifying upstream denial risk before claims are submitted.

Built by Kori Pickle to demonstrate denial prevention thinking, claim readiness review, revenue cycle workflow analysis, and remote-ready healthcare operations documentation.

Recruiter Quick Read View Dashboard

No PHI / Synthetic Data Notice

All data shown in this project is synthetic and created for portfolio demonstration purposes. No patient information, protected health information, or real payer case data is used.

Core Revenue Cycle Insight

Denials do not begin at claim follow-up. Many denials are born upstream in eligibility, documentation, authorization, coding readiness, and claim preparation.

Recruiter Quick Read

A 60-second overview of who built this, what it solves, and what it proves.

Who built this?

Kori Pickle, Healthcare Administration student focused on revenue cycle workflow analysis, denial prevention, patient access risk, and healthcare operations.

What problem does it solve?

DPIS identifies upstream denial risk before claims are submitted and before preventable defects become rework, delayed reimbursement, or revenue leakage.

What skills does it prove?

Root-cause analysis, KPI design, claim readiness review, denial risk scoring, executive reporting, synthetic data design, and workflow documentation.

What roles does it support?

Denials Analyst, Revenue Cycle Analyst, Revenue Integrity Analyst, Patient Access Analyst, Claims Follow-Up Representative, and Healthcare Operations Analyst roles.
